Pork Chops

These pork chops are perfectly seasoned and brined. This recipe is a great choice for someone looking for meals for their busy work week. To get started gather the following:

  • 3lb pork chops
  • 3Tb of rosemary
  • 2 white onions
  • 1/2 cup of salt
  • 4 cups of Water

Follow the following steps to make these pork chops:

  1. Take out pork chops and put them into a container with the water, salt, and rosemary
  2. Let sit overnight
  3. Cut onions into long slices
  4. Put pork chops and onions in the oven for 25 minutes at 400 degrees. Cover the pork shops with the onions
  5. Take out and enjoy!

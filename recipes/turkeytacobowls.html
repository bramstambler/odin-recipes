To make the best turkey taco bowls you've ever tasted start by gathering the following ingredients:

  • 1lb turkey
  • 4oz cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 whole tomato
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 can of corn

Follow the following steps to complete the dish:

  1. Cook turkey in pan on medium high heat until cooked throughly
  2. Cook rice
  3. Mix ingredients

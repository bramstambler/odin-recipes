Perfect Cookies

This recipe must be tasted to believe! These cookies make the most soft gooey delicious cookies you've ever tasted. Each batch makes 30 cookies. To begin you must start with gathering the following ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 packs vanilla pudding mix
  • 16 ounces of milk chocolate chips

To perpare begin:
  1. Mix the ingredients in no particular order
  2. Place in the fridge overnight
  3. Put the cookie dough in properly sized pieces into the oven at 350 for 12 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
